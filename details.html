Location

Uptown Indigo is an event venue with chic, industrial design located in Historic Uptown Shelby, North Carolina and makes up two floors and the rooftop of the 1927 Campbell Building. It offers a captivating setting for weddings and upscale private functions. It is located at 112 N. LAFAYETTE ST, SHELBY, NC 28150, US.

Shelby, NC is located about 45 minutes along I-85 west of Charlotte Douglas International Airport and is at the confluence of the beautiful rolling foothills of the Blue Ridge Mountains and the bustling Metrolina region of the Carolinas.
